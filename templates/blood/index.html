About LifeSource Blood Bank

Bridging the gap between those who need blood and those who can donate LifeSource Blood Bank is committed to saving lives.

LifeSource Blood Bank is a non-profit organization dedicated to ensuring that no life is lost due to a lack of available blood.

Our mission is to create a seamless, efficient, and reliable blood donation network accessible to all.

We partner with hospitals, medical institutions, and community organizations to maintain a safe blood supply. Using our advanced system, we quickly match donors with recipients and keep track of blood stocks.
